Step 1 - Explore

We offer multiple courses in three flexible packages. Take your time, explore, and choose what suits you best. If it feels overwhelming, don’t worry—we’re here to guide you.

Oh, and if you’re feeling extra ambitious, yes—you can enroll in more than one course. No one's stopping you!

Step 2 - Finalize & Pay

By now, you’ve likely found the course that fits you best. Each one comes in three tailored packages, each with its own perks—choose what works for you and let’s get started.

Step 3 - Interactive Live Classes

No boring pre-recorded videos here. If you’re committing your time, we’re committing ours. Our live classes are engaging, interactive, and—dare we say—fun? Just like the robot on the left. (Go ahead, give it a try with your mouse)

Step 4 - Assessments

Throughout your course, you’ll take periodic assessments to track your progress. These aren’t just tests—they help us understand where you shine and where we can guide you better.

Step 5 - Analyze & Aid

As you near the end of your course, we'll analyze multiple factors—including your performance, soft skills, and strengths—to craft a TagProfile for you.​

From there, you'll receive 1-on-1 coaching to refine your skills, boost your confidence, and ensure you're fully prepared to step into the job market.​

Step 6 - Land The Job?

We’ll polish your CV and make sure it stands out—so you walk in prepared, confident, and ready to impress.​

Our team will push your applications to our hiring partners, ensuring your profile reaches the right desks. Your journey doesn’t end with the course. We’re with you until you get hired.​
